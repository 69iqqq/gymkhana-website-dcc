About the event

The 2024 Industry-Academia Conclave aims to foster collaboration between academia and industry to address critical concerns, promote interaction, knowledge sharing, and solve challenges for mutual benefit.

Venue: Diamond Jubilee Lecture Hall Complex (DJLHC) NITJSR

Date: 15th -17th Nov

OBJECTIVES

  1. Enhance industry-academia connections for future growth.
  2. Offer advanced research and technology to tackle industrial challenges.
  3. Develop industry-relevant academic programs and shared facilities.
  4. Promote entrepreneurship through startup support, mentorship, and funding access.

आत्मनिर्भर भारत

National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur being an institute of national importance is committed to play its prime duty towards Atmanirbhar Bharat
